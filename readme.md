**MindStock** is a stock market simulation game that reveals your investment psychology. Make split-second buy and sell decisions under real-time market pressure, and discover what kind of investor you truly are. Inspired by the fast-paced nature of short-form content and the popularity of personality type tests, **MindStock** analyzes your trading behavior to generate a personalized investment tendency type — because in the market, your instincts speak louder than your intentions.
